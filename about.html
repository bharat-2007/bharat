About BMW

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG (BMW) is a German luxury automobile and motorcycle manufacturer founded in 1916. Headquartered in Munich, Germany, BMW is known for its high-performance vehicles, innovative engineering, and premium design. The company produces a range of cars and motorcycles under the BMW, Mini, and Rolls-Royce brands. BMW's signature kidney grille and advanced technology, such as its EfficientDynamics and iDrive systems, have made it a leader in the automotive industry.

The brand is also heavily involved in motorsports and electric vehicle development, with models like the BMW i series showcasing its commitment to sustainability and innovation.
